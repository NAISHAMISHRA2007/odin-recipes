Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is hearty, cheesy, and perfect for a family dinner. It's layered with a rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and gooey mozzarella.

Making this dish takes time and love, but the end result is a comforting favorite that everyone will enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the ground beef with onion and garlic until browned.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Boil lasagna noodles until al dente.
  5. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella.
  6. Repeat layers and top with Parmesan cheese.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  8. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
